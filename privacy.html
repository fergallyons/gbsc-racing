Privacy Policy

Galway Bay Sailing Club — GBSC Racing App
Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

Galway Bay Sailing Club ("GBSC", "we", "us") operates the GBSC Racing App, a progressive web application used to manage race registrations, course publishing, crew rosters and race fee collection for club racing events.

GBSC is the data controller for personal data collected through this app. For any privacy-related queries contact us at: secretary@gbsc.ie

2. What personal data we collect

  • Boat name — entered by the skipper at registration.
  • Crew names (first and last) — entered by the skipper when building a crew roster.
  • Phone numbers — optionally entered by the skipper for crew members, used to send race fee payment reminders via WhatsApp.
  • Membership type and year joined — used to calculate the correct race fee.
  • Number of outings — tracked for visitor crew members to manage the club's visitor outing limit.

We do not collect email addresses, home addresses, payment card details or any special category data.

3. Why we collect it and our lawful basis

We rely on legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) as our lawful basis for processing. GBSC has a genuine operational need to manage race entries, collect race fees and communicate with crew members about payments. The data collected is limited to what is strictly necessary for these purposes.

4. How we use the data

  • To display crew rosters and calculate race fees on race night.
  • To send payment reminders to crew members via WhatsApp (only where a phone number has been provided).
  • To track visitor outing counts against the club's membership policy.
  • To produce race fee reports for the Race Officer and club treasurer.

We do not use personal data for marketing, profiling or any automated decision-making.

5. Who we share it with

Personal data is stored in Supabase (a cloud database provider using AWS infrastructure in the EU). Supabase acts as our data processor and operates under a Data Processing Agreement that meets GDPR requirements.

Race fee payments made by card are processed by Stripe, which operates under its own privacy policy. GBSC does not receive or store card details.

We do not sell, rent or share personal data with any third party for their own purposes.

6. How long we keep it

Crew records are retained for the duration of the sailing season and reviewed annually. Race fee submission records are retained for a minimum of one year for accounting purposes. Data no longer needed for club operations is deleted.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data where there is no legitimate reason to continue holding it.
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at secretary@gbsc.ie. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission: www.dataprotection.ie

8. Security

Data is stored in a secured cloud database with access controls in place. Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS. Access to the app requires a boat-specific PIN. We do not store passwords.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
